# Origami Shield With Cross - Instructions

### Step 1
This is the front of our paper, the background of the shield will end up this colour.

### Step 2
This is the back of our origami paper, this colour will become the shape of the cross.

### Step 3
Fold the paper up in half.

### Step 4
Unfold the previous step.

### Step 5
Fold the right side over to the left side.

### Step 6
Unfold the previous step.

### Step 7
Using the creases as guidelines, fold one of the corner to the middle, leaving a small gap.

### Step 8
Fold the rest of the corners in to match the first one.

### Step 9
Next, fold the the top edge over to the back. You can decide upon the positioning of this fold.

### Step 10
Fold the left and right sides behind as well.

### Step 11
Here is the back of the paper.

### Step 12
You can fold the top left and right points and the bottom edge behind a little to round out your shield.
