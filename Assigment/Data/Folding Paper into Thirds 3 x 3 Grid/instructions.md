# Grid Setup 3x3 - Instructions

### Step 1
This is the front of the paper, your divider will end up being this color.

### Step 2
This is the back of the paper, usually white.

### Step 3
You will need to divide the paper into thirds. This is the 'S' method, pick up the paper and make it an S shape, until the paper becomes equally divided into thirds.

### Step 4
Once it's in three equal sections, make a crease on both edges. Alternatively, get a ruler and measure thirds along the top edge of the paper with pencil.

### Step 5
Place the paper back down on the table. You can see the thirds marked on the top edge.

### Step 6
Fold the right edge over to the mark on the top left.

### Step 7
Unfold the previous step.

### Step 8
Fold the left edge over to the crease you made in the previous step.

### Step 9
Unfold the previous step.

### Step 10
Next, fold the bottom right corner diagonally, aligning with the right vertical crease.

### Step 11
Fold the top edge down to align with the top of the flap you created in the previous step.

### Step 12
Unfold both the top and lower diagonal flap.

### Step 13
Fold the bottom edge up to the horizontal crease you made in the previous step.

### Step 14
Unfold the paper. You now have a grid of 3 by 3, or 9 equal sections.
