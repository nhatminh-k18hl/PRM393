# Rabbit Ear Fold - Instructions

### Step 1
Start with your paper like this.

### Step 2
Fold the top point down to the bottom point.

### Step 3
Unfold the previous step.

### Step 4
Take the top right diagonal edge and fold it down, aligning with the crease you made in step 2.

### Step 5
Unfold the previous step.

### Step 6
Repeat the same fold on the top left diagonal edge.

### Step 7
Unfold the previous step.

### Step 8
Create a new vertical valley fold at the top whilst pushing top left and right edges forward.

### Step 9
Flatten the paper like this.
